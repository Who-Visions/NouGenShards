# Commercial Use

NouGenShards is intended for personal and non-commercial use by default.

## 🤝 Defining Commercial Use
Commercial use includes, but is not limited to:
- Selling a product or service that includes NouGenShards code.
- Using NouGenShards to provide paid consulting or agent services.
- Implementing NouGenShards within a corporate environment for internal business processes.

## 🔑 How to get Commercial Permission
If you would like to use NouGenShards commercially, you have two paths:

1.  **Who Visions Pro Subscription**: Our "Pro" and "Team" plans grant you limited commercial usage rights for your individual or small team workflows.
2.  **Enterprise Licensing**: For deeper integration or redistribution, please contact us for a custom license agreement.

## 🛡️ Why we do this
We believe in building a sustainable business. By keeping the "Intelligence Layer" and commercial rights proprietary, we can continue to invest in the local-first memory substrate that the whole community uses for free.
